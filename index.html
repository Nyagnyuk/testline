The Investing Process
How we do it
1
Present to us

Send us your proposal digitally, we’ll review it as quickly as we can!

2
We talk

If it’s of interest, we’ll contact you to find out further information.

3
Offer stage

Once we feel we have what we need, if we like what we see, we’ll make you a non-binding offer.

4
Due diligence

This is where we analyse under a microscope, checking the facts, figures and legalities of everything proposed.

5
Closing

We agree on a set of closing documents; we sign on the dotted line, and we move forward!
